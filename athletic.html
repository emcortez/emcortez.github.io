This Fall Believe In... Promotional Series

August 2023, Adobe InDesign

In one of my first independent projects, I created a series of digital signage to promote the fall sports for USF Dons. I specifically chose poses that showed character and defined the vibe of each sport, to increase excitement for the sports this Fall semester. A total of 8 rotating posters were displayed at the Sobrato Center and on various screens throughout campus.

One of my biggest tasks is creating email header designs for email marketing campaigns. From promoting gamedays to specific student promotions, my designs are meant to grab the attention and give specific details about the game.

Social Media

Adobe Photoshop, InDesign
